Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid.

Players take turns marking spaces with their symbol (X or O) and the first to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.

If all spaces are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw
